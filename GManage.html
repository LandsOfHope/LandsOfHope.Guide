Manage / Create
The Manage / Create screen is the center piece of managing the guild, for new players it is also where you create a guild.

There are so many features here that the screen has been broken down into many tabs, the amount of tabs visible to you depends on your guild permissions and those can be set via the Edit Titles screen.

Roster
Lists all of the members of your guild.

Treasury
Lets you access the guild Treasury and put funds in or take funds out as needed.

Guild
You can edit the Guild Name and Contact Details and some guild options via this screen.

Symbol
Lets you change the symbol that is used on Guild screens, there are over 120 symbols to choose from and together with a guild color give you somewhat of a uniqueness.

Color
Lets you change your guild color, this color is used on Guild screens but also when combined with the actual guild color reward item you can change the color that your name is displayed in to other players.

Notes
Can be used to view Guild Notices, these are important notices on various aspects of your guild such as when a member is hired or leaves.

Members
Lets you edit existing members, promote them and alter various settings for them, it can also be used to Message a member or Kick someone from the guild.

New Recruits
Same as the Members screen only this time it shows new recruits, and lets you promote them to a full member of the guild or Kick them out..

Titles
You can use this screen to add or edit titles, setting the permissions each title gets and thus how a member of this tile can interact with the features of the guild.

Bonuses
Lets you view the Bonuses the Guild has unlocked via Guild Questor NPCs.

Rewards
Lets you view the Rewards the Guild has unlocked via Guild Questor NPCs.

Add Members
You can use this screen to send out invites to add new members to your guild. A player receiving the invite can refuse to join, but if they choose to join they will show up on the New Recruits screen.
