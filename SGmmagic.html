Gm Magic
The GM MAGIC skill determines what spells you can learn in this line of magic, it also contributes to the effect of any spell cast in this spell line.

To learn more spells you need to find Spell Books or visit a Trainer who can teach you more spells.

Your magic lines allow you to cast spells in and out of combat.

This skill allows you to cast spells such as:
  • Phoenix: Flight I
  • Change into a Sheep
  • Human Female I
  • Wizard I
  • Giant Frog: Dexterity I
