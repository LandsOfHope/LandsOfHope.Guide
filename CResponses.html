Responses
The Responses screen can be used to setup short lines of text that will be displayed automatically by your character when interacting with the world around you, when talking to NPCs and when fighting in combat.

There are various different responses:

NOTHING
This is the default response that is shown when someone tries to talk with you.

Example:
Farmer Bob: Hail and well met Ralph

NOTHING
This response may be used at times when there is no other available response, in particular when looking for a quest from an NPC which is out of quests.

Example:
Farmer Bob: I dont have anything for you right now, sorry.

FIGHT
This response is shown at the start of any fight, great for taunting your enemies.

Example:
Farmer Bob: You may kill me but I will come back stronger than ever before !

ENEMYTALK
This response is shown when someone from an enemy allegiance tries to talk with you.

Example:
Farmer Bob: Ger`rout of my fields and don`t come back, we dont want scum like you here abouts!

STOLENFROM
This response is shown when someone steals from you, as you cant steal from Players on non-grief servers this mainly applies to the grief servers.

Example:
Farmer Bob: I`m gonna go get a lynch mob and we will come back and hang you!

WIN, LOSE, BUY, SELL
Somewhat under construction and not properly implemented yet but they are intended to be used when Winning/Losing in a fight, or when items are Bought or Sold to you (great for Vendors or Town NPCs).

HISTORY
The History to be displayed when someone uses the history button on your character.

Example:
I was born and raised in a small house on the edge of town, my father was a cripple and a drunk and my mother died from the plague!
