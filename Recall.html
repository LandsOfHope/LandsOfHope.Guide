Recall
The Recall system is one of the great features of the game, with the Recall system you can mark various tiles or rooms as you come across them and these are then added to your Travel screen.

Within the Travel Screen you then get a view of the realm your in with your Recall points displayed so that you can manage them or recall to them.

As it may take a few seconds to move from a point on the mini-map to a point off the mini-map the recall system was created so that it would be faster and also so you did not have to memorise and enter coordinates to move around.

Anyone can recall there are no restrictions on doing so and there is no limit to the amount of times you can recall.

You can also turn on the Travel Extra Bar and then you will get a list of all of your "Quick" enabled recall points on the right of the Game Window, meaning you can click on one to travel across the map instantly.
