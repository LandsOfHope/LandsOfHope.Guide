Ready Spell
The Ready Spell screen allows you to flag certain spells as "readied" you can also use this screen to unready spells.

A Readied spell is a spell that is used automatically in combat when you click the Cast Offensive/Defensive readied spell buttons.

It is wise to ready a few spells at different mana and intelligence requirements as during a fight your mana will go down so you may not get the chance to cast your most powerful spells more than once.

By readying a good range of spells you will be able to cast a number of spells automatically without having to manually select them.
