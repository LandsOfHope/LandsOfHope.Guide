Beasts
The Track Beasts screen can be used to track anything that is classified as a Beast, this screen uses the Track Beasts skill.



As with the other track or map screens it shows a view of the current area in the current realm you can use the Arrow buttons to move the view around the realm.

This screen will let you track active spawns, that is monsters that are currently spawned or players/pets that are currently in the area.

You can alter the level range that you wish to track, the maximum level range you can track is based on your track beasts skill, and the more things you track the more skill you will get.

Click on the marked beast to goto its location.
