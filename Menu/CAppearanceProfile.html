Appearance Profile
The Appearance Profile screen can be used to alter aspects of your character, you can use this screen to alter your Name, Title and Picture.

If you have bought a new picture, title or name these will be listed here along with your old details and at any time you can switch your Name, Title or Picture around with previously bought details, it allows you to mix and match so you could take the title from one screen and mix it with the name from another and the picture from yet another.

Any Slayer Titles gained can also be activated/switched via this screen.
