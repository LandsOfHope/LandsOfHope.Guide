Trade Bags
The Trade Bag screen lists all of the items you have in your Trade Bag. Your trade bag is a special part of your inventory that is extremely secure, you can not put things into your trade bag and nor can things be stolen from it. Typically when trading with another character and sometimes when buying particular items or bought extras the item will be moved to your trade bag, this is so you do not lose or mislay the item.

You can remove the item from your trade bag using this screen.
