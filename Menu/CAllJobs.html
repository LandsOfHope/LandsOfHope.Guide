All Jobs
The All Jobs screen is basically a search and listing screen, it displays all of the Jobs in the system and lets you search within those jobs for specific jobs.

You can also use the Browse button on this screen to view a breakdown of all of the jobs by the Skill that is required to make the item.

If you wish to complete one of these jobs or view more information on the job, simply click on it and you will have the opportunity.

When you complete a job you are paid the amount of money that the player was offering and you lose the item from your Inventory so be careful as its possible you may accidentally hand over an item you were planning on keeping.

The screen that lets you complete the job lists some of the items you have in your Inventory that match the Job, you can then choose which item you wish to hand in, so that the game doesnt take the wrong item.
