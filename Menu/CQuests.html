Quests
The Quests screen can be used to view the quests you are currently on, all of the incomplete quests are shown on this screen in an easy to read format.

To get more information on a Quest you can simply click on it, you may also be able to forfeit the quest from this screen.

If you wish to view a list of quests you have previously completed you can use the Completed button at the bottom of the screen to access that information.
