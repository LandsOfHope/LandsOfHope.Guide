Info
The Info screen displays indepth information about your Character, a lot of information is condensed into one screen there are many tabs on this screen which let you view different bits of information.

Info
The main tab it displays your name, race, profession and level etc ...

Group
Displays a quick summary of group information for your Character.

Guild
Displays a quick summary of guild information for your Character.

Points
Displays a number of important points such as Kills, Deaths and Player Kills.

Pets
Displays a short list of the pets you have, there is a limit on the amount of pets shown here so if your pet is not visible it just means its over the limit.

Property
Displays a short list of the property/buildings you own, again this list is limited so may not show all property if you own a lot.

Skills
Displays a short list of about the 20 highest skills you have.

Equipment
Displays 25 of the items you currently have equipped.

At the bottom of the screen you will find several buttons to perform specific tasks.

Stealth or Unstealth
Allows your Character to stealth or unstealth, only works if you have Stealth skill.

Disguise
Allows your Character to put on a disguise, only works if you have Disguise skill.

Public or Private
Makes you appear or disappear on public Character screens, these are screens that any player can use to get you to do a task for them such as the Craft Marketeer, Locksmith or Appraiser. If you wish to earn extra money it is wise to stay public but if you do not wish people to use your skills to do things in the game then go private.

Dismount
If you are riding a mount, you can click this button to dismount.

Dismount
If you are riding a mount, you can click this button to dismount.

Aggressive or Defensive
If you have Taunting skill you can set your Aggressive or Defensive status which determines who gets attacked in combat, without Taunting skill when attacking a monster the monster attacks someone in your fight group randomly.

History
Allows you to view the Character History that was entered via the Responses screen.
