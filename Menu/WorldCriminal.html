Criminal
The Track Criminals screen can be used to track anything that has a bounty, this screen uses the Track and Capture criminals skill.





As with the other track or map screens it shows a view of the current area in the current realm you can use the Arrow buttons to move the view around the realm.

This screen will only let you track players or pets with a bounty and not monsters.

You can alter the level range that you wish to track, the maximum level range you can track is based on your track and capture criminals skill, and the more things you track the more skill you will get.

Click on the marked criminal to goto its location.
