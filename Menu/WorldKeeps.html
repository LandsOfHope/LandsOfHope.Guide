Keeps
The Keeps screen can be used to quickly check on the status of the keeps in the Battlefield map, you do not have to be in the battlefield to use this screen.

You can not get to the battlefield until you are level 1000 and have done your allegiance quests.



The Keeps are marked on the map and the map uses grids, each grid is colored to suit the allegiance that is holding the grid.

ExileThe Exiles own this keep.
SocietyThe Society owns this keep.
ReaperThe Reapers own this keep.
No colorNo allegiance owns this keep.
