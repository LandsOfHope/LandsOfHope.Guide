Patches
The Patches screen can be used to fit Inventory Patches into items, the screen lists the patches you currently have in your inventory and then lets you use them with subsequent screens listing the items you can fit the patch into.

There are two types of patches at the moment, Armor patches which can be fitted into pieces of armor to improve the armor rating and Fury patches which can be fitted into weapons to improve the attack power rating.

Patches can only be gained through completing challenges although additional ways of gaining patches may be added at a later date.

Only one patch can be in an item at any one time if you try to fit another patch then the new patch will overwrite the old patch, if you remove a patch the patch is destroyed.
