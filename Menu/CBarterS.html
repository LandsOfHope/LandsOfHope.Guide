Barter Status
The Barter Status screen is the only way to administer/check the status of a Barter, once you have initiated a Barter with another player this screen is the way to accept or decline the trade.

This screen will display all of the items in the Barter as well as the money wanted by the person doing the bartering, it will also display if any items in this Barter are no longer held by the barterer, such as if they sold an item or dropped one after setting the barter details.

A Barter is non negotiable, after one is made it has to be deleted and re-made if you wish to change its price or the items being bartered. This is so that a player will always get what they were bartering for and not get scammed.
