Beastmastery
The Beastmastery skill allows you to tame beasts to act as your minion

This skill also allows you to cast spells such as:
  • Posess: Fish
  • Self Harm
  • Posess: Chicken
  • Posess: Frog
  • Posess: Bird


Skill Use
Find a Monster classified as a Beast, move the mouse over the monster and click the Tame button. If you have enough skill the Beast will be tamed as your latest minion.

To cast spells use the Abilities > Spellbook screen.
