Travel
The Travel screen shows you your marked locations, whenever you mark a location it will show up on this screen.

As the screen displays an area of the current realm you may need to use the Arrow buttons to move the view around to a spot where you marked a location.

You can use the 2 Letter abbreviation buttons in the middle of the screen to switch to another realm view.



You can use this screen to recall to a marked location, it is also the screen to use for administering your marked locations.

Recall
You teleport to that location.

Remove
Remove this marked location, it will no longer be marked.

Rename
Rename the marker, call it whatever you want.

Quick On
Turns this marked location on, meaning it appears on your Travel Extra Bar.

Quick Off
Turns this marked location off, meaning it does not appear on your Travel Extra Bar.

Image
Allows you to change the small image that this marked location will use on the Travel Extra Bar and some other screens.
