Active Effects
To the left of the Character Details area is the Active Effects display, this is where it shows any spells currently in effect on your character as well as other abilities.

This is also the place where the "Recall to Quarters" link for new players appears.



The Spells are displayed with the time they have left below them, some spells can be cancelled simply by clicking on the spell, others may not be cancelled and have to run out naturally.

There is a limit of only 8 spells in effect at any one time, although some special spells and things like traps can still be applied ontop of this.
