Buying
Throughout Myzan there are many NPCs, while not all of them will let you buy items from them the majority of NPCs inside towns aswell as Healers will usually offer you something to buy.

When you click on an NPC it will usually either show the Sell Items screen (the screen you can use to sell your junk to the NPC) or it may show another screen, in both cases at the bottom there may be a "Buy Items" button if you click this then it will load the Buying window.



You will know the Buying screen when you see it as it will be called something like "Buy Items from NPC Name" and will show all kinds of weird and wonderful items.

The Buying screen itself groups the items the NPC has for sale into categories such as 1 Handed Weapons and 2 Handed Shields, you can browse through the list of goods for sale by clicking on the category header, and also the page numbers at the top of the screen to move to another page if this NPC has more than 1 page worth of goods.

If you see something you wish to buy, click on the Item and it will show more information about it and present you with an Info button you can use to find out even more information, if you decide you wish to buy this item you can then click the Buy button and if you have the funds and the inventory space the item will be bought.

Depending on the NPC you are buying from a bought item will either goto your Backpack or your Tradebag, it will usually tell you where the item went if it does not expressly state that it went to a specific bag it will have most likely been placed in your backpack.
