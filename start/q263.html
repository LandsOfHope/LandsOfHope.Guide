Training - Casting
Mage Bromly at Point C has a quest that all classes can take but only Healers and Casters will find a use for. If you complete the quest you will gain an Estate Spellbook.

To use the book, goto your Inventory > Containers screen click on the Estate Spellbook item and then click the Use button you will then learn the 3 lowest spells your profession can gain.

Yyou should goto World > Rest to ensure your Health and Stamina is at 100% after changing your equipment and then go speak to Patricia at Point B.
