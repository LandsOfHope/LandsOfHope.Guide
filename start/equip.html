Equipping
Goto the Inventory menu at the top of the screen, then click on the Equipment link to open the Equipment screen.

The Equipment screen has a paperdoll on the left, allowing you to click on a slot to view a list of the items you can equip to that slot, alternatively you can use the slot bar (on the right of the screen) they both represent all of the slots that items can be equipped to, most of these will be greyed out. If a slot is greyed out then you dont have any items in your inventory, also if the paperdoll has a red border around a slot this means you have nothing equipped to it, and is intended as a reminder.

Depending on how far along you are in the Estate Quests you should have items in your Leg and Chest slots, if you have the Drakos Brand then you will also have an item to equip in one of the two Bonus slot.

When you click on an available slot, the items you have will be displayed, clicking on an item will equip it or unequip it. Items that show up purple on this screen are already equipped.

Note: After equipping goto World > Rest to regenerate your Hitpoints/Mana and Stamina to full to reflect the equipment changes.
