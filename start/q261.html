Training - Combat
Guide Derwa gives you the Estate Supply Box after completing the Training - Combat series of quests.

To use the box, goto your Inventory > Containers screen click on the Estate Supply Box item and then click the Use button the contents of the box will be deposited in your Inventory.

The items in the box depend entirely on what Class you picked when you started the game, but typically it will contain armor/clothing and weapons.

You should goto the Inventory > Equipment screen to equip your new items more ...

If you are a caster or healer you should take the Casting quest (linked below) before attempting to fight anything, otherwise you should goto World > Rest to ensure your Health and Stamina is at 100% after changing your equipment and then go speak to Patricia at Point B.
