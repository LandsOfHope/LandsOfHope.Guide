Options
The Options screen can be used to configure many of the advanced features of the game such as turning Music and Sounds on/off, turning on the Level map or turning off Lighting.

There are a lot of Options and some are pretty useful in configuring the game to act the way you want it to, the Options screen is made up of many different groups or tabs and each tab has a series of options you can configure.

The Options that appear in red on this screen require the browser window to be refreshed after being changed.
