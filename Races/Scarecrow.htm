Classification: Humanoid
Appearance
Tall and stuffed with straw, Scarecrows are often used to guard the gardens of farms. They are dressed in the worn and tattered clothing of the farm owners. These rags give the scarecrow a very spooky appearance. Often they are thought to be watching those who travel near them and many believe that when they are assembled, they can move about.
