Classification: Undead
Appearance
The shadow in this form appears as a simple shadow, able to contort itself into another beings shadow to hide, stalk and kill its prey. It has the ability to take solid form, the appearance of a lesser demon
General
Shadows are a unique branch of the demonic realm. They can exist both as a physical manifestation of itself, or as a simple creature of pure shadow. Generally running into either is a very bad thing.
History
Shadows were originally thought to be undead, similar to a ghost. Further study into their nature revealed they are actually demons, a form of shapeshifter of the demonic planes. They have stalked and killed many through the centuries.
