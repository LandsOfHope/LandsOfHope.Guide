Classification: Beast
Appearance
Deer are four legged animals that stand smaller than the average horse.with the male deer being larger than the female. They come in a variety of colors and shades, mostly reds and browns. The young calves are born spotted to help them blend into their surrounding but these spots fade as they age. The male deer is unique in that he bears a bony growth called antlers on his head. These make formidable weapons for the deer which he uses to protect his territory.

General
Deer are shy animals that dwell in many areas of Myzan. They can be found deep in the forests, on the meadows, feeding on the grassy areas and even crossing the great frozen lakes.
History
Deer have always been a part of the animal world of Myzan. They have long been used to provide food and clothing for the humans. Beautiful prepared hides have many uses, weapons, clothing, blankets and even linings for storage chests.

At one time in long ago history, the deer was declared ro be a royal animal and only royal hunts with the King and his followers were allowed.
