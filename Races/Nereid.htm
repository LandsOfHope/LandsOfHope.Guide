Classification: Humanoid
Appearance
They are a shy species, but the ones people have reported all appear to have feminine features. Their skins are soft pastels of blue and green to blend in with their aquatic surroundings. While not apparently strong, if provoked they will fight fiercely.
General
Nereid are an aquatic species of Myzan. They are humanoid and generally keep to themselves. They will fight to defend their homes though, or if forced by the larger predatory species of the waters such as sea daemons.
History
Generally thought to be a relation to pixies, not much is actually known about the Nereid. Attempts at communications with them by explorers and sailors have failed. It is known though they predate mermaid stories, leading some to believe they are what mermaids evolve from.
