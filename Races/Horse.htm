Classification: Beast
Appearance
Horses have long and muscular legs that propel the beasts at amazing speeds. Their bodies are stocky and long like a wine barrel. Horses have long necks and a long face with eyes on the sides of the head. This allows them to see behind them. The hair of horses can be just about any color.
General
Strong and noble, Horses are the mount of choice for many of the citizens of Myzan. These large creatures can also be worthy opponents to adventurers young and old. Horses often travel in herds called harems and are fiercely territorial. Wandering into the vicinity of a harem of horses can be hazardous to anyone�s health.
