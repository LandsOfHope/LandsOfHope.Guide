Expansion Pack: Jurassic Wars
Classification: Beast
Appearance
Growing up to eight feet long, and close to nine hundred pounds, the sabretooth tiger is a dangerous predator. Their two prominent canines, more like daggers then teeth, can grow up to nearly a foot long and combined with their jaw strength, can pierce most non metallic armors. They are powerfully built, and if tamed can even support heavy riders.
General
One of the few mammals that have survived in Juratania. These large cats are master hunters. Their pack society can take down even the most stubborn of prey. The Eversight Expedition keeps constant watch for ambushes from these large cats.
