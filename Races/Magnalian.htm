Classification: Humanoid
Appearance
A Magnalian has a rather strange beauty. Their skins are scaled like dragons and come in a variety of shades of red. Bright yellow spikes top their head giving an appearance of hair. Their most striking feature are their brilliant blue eyes which seem to entrance their victims.
General
Magnalians live in the hottest areas of Myzan. Dwelling on lava and magma, they make their homes where nothing else could live. They are resistant to many of the weapons humans employ be it magical or mundane.
History
Legend has it that the Magnalian race was born during a wild lightning storm. It is said that a piece of the heavens fell to the earth and landed in the heart of the magma just as a spike of lightning struck. Created with the fire in their souls they have found little reason to step outside their territories and have had little true interaction with the human race.
