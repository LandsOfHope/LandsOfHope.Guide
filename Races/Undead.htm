Classification: Undead
Appearance
These undead are generally in some state of decomposition, rangeing from skin and hair falling off, to whole limbs. These particular ones are mindless slaves, only meant to serve the Reaper elite. Scraps of tattered clothing hangs off their forms.
General
Undead, they can range from simple reanimated corpses, to highly intelligent Liches. When generally speaking about undead, you are talking about the massive undead hoardes of the Reapers. The reanimated footsoldiers that make up the bulk of their forces. These undead can not be reasoned or bargined with.
History
For as long as man has delved into the mysteries of life and extending it, the undead have walked Myzan. It is believed that the first undead to be created resulted in an arcane experiment involving resurrection. The practice of Necromancy is what brings about most of this type of undead in todays Myzan. Though occasionally a lone Reaper will turn a whole town into these for some unknown purpose.
