Items
The Track Items screen can be used to track for specific types of items on the map, it is a skill only the Crime Lord profession gets.



As with the other track or map screens it shows a view of the current area in the current realm you can use the Arrow buttons to move the view around the realm.

At the right side of the screen is a series of buttons corresponding to the type of items you can track, right now Track Items lets you track treasure bags, herbs, treasure maps in bottles and treasure chests.

Click on the marked item to goto its location, or click anywhere on the map to goto that location.
