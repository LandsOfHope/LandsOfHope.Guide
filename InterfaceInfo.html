Character Details
At the top left of the screen just below the Menu is the Character Details display, it shows important information like Health, Mana, Experience, Level and Character name.



If you are riding a mount the picture of the mount appears in the top right corner of the Details area, alternatively if you are posessing a minion or monster then a picture of your Character will appear here.

In both cases if you click the picture you will either dismount (if its your mount) or return to your normal body if you were posessed.
