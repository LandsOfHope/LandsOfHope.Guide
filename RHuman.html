Lands of Hope features an evolution system which allows a player to evolve into other races when they reach certain level and stat requirements.

There are Five tiers of evolution, Tier I is your starting race. Tier II your level 200 evolution, Tier III your level 400 evolution, Tier IV your level 600 evolution and Tier V your level 800 evolution.

Evolution is Linear once you have evolved at level 200 you can only evolve along that line or choose to not evolve of course.
