Friends
The Friends screen displays all of the players in the game that you have classed as a friend, friends are people that you wish to have closer communication with and may wish to follow their progress through the use of the Friends Extrabar.

Adding someone to your friends screen will allow them to appear on your Message screen as a message recipient as well as on the Post screens as a post recipient without you have to search through a list of players to find them.

You can use the Add Friend button at the bottom of the screen to add a new friend.

The friends screen has options to do the following:

Remove
Remove someone from your friends list.

Info
View full Information about this person.

Follow
Enable following on the selected friend, if following is turned on you will follow the friend when they move.

This is a feature best used when you log out and your friend is meant to be levelling you.

Message
Send a message to the selected friend.

Follow
Enable following on the selected friend, if following is turned on you will follow the friend when they move.

This is a feature best used when you log out and your friend is meant to be levelling you.

Ban or Unban
By default people added to your friends list start off banned, this restricts the information they can see about you when using their Friend screen clicking the Unban button will then put them as a friend.

Hide or Unhide
Hides certain details from this Friend such as your Online/Offline status and location.

Watch or Unwatch
Adds or removes this friend from your Watch list, your watch list is what is displayed if you have the Friends Extrabar turned on.
