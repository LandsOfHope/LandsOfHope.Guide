Dismantle
The Dismantle screen can be used to dismantle items that are craftable, when dismantling an item you get part of the resources that went into making the item back again.

You can choose to fast dismantle or slow dismantle.

Fast Dismantle
The item is dismantled instantly you get no xp but you get the resource back immediately.

Slow Dismantle
The item is dismantled via the queue system, it will take 15 seconds but you will get the resource and some experience.
