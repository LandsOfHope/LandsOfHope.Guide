Salvager
The Salvager is often regarded as someone who has no thought for preserving the relics of the past, they would rather smash a priceless vase on their hunt for gold and jewels than preserve it for future generations.

Salvagers can deconstruct items using the Deconstruction skill (starting skill), they can also Salvage corpses once they are out of the estate to gain additional resources.

Salvagers get a 10% looted money increase and access to Tier 1 Salvage goods.
