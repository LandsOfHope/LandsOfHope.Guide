**Monster drops**  
The following items can be gained from monster drops:

*   Horde treasure map
*   Wurmscale (wurm only)
*   Dragonstone (dragons only)
*   Pile of ash

**Random world items**  
These items may be found randomly while exploring Eastern Lands.

*   Pile of dragon dung
