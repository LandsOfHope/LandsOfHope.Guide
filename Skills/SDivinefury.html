Divine Fury
The DIVINE FURY skill represents your ability to deal aditional holy damage while in the heat of battle.

This skill is a feature of the Crusader class, every time you take damage (physical damage and not magical damage) you gain more rage, your divine fury skill determines how much rage you can get, for each point in Divine Fury you can have 1pt in Rage. Rage is then added back onto your physical damage.

Note: Actions such as being healed or resting will lower your Rage points.

The higher the skill the more damage you can potentially deal.

Formula:
Damage Dealt + RANDOM(RAGE POINTS)

This skill is used in conjunction with your other combat abilities.
