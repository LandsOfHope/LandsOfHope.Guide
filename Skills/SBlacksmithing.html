Blacksmithing
The BLACKSMITHING crafting skill, alllows you to craft items. You can access the crafting screen via the Inventory > Tradeskill > blacksmithing menu.

This skill allows you to craft items such as:
  • throwing knife
  • stick
  • dagger
  • short staff
  • club


As with all of the crafting skills you will need to learn plans for the blacksmithing skill, seek out a plan trainer for this skill or buy dropped plans from other players.

All Items made with this skill are added to your Inventory > Queue screen, so check this screen frequently to complete your items.
