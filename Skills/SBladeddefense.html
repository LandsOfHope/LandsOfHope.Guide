Bladed Defense
The BLADED DEFENSE skill enables you to defend against more easily against attacks from the following skills:
  • AXES
  • DAGGERS
  • SWORDS
  • TWO-HANDED AXES
  • TWO-HANDED SWORDS

Formula:
Damage Reduction = (BLADED DEFENSE / 10)

This means for every 10 points in this skill you absorb 1pt of damage.
