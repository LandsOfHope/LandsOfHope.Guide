Appraisal
The Appraisal skill is used to learn more about the world we live in.

It can be used to Identify unidentified items that you may find on your travels. It can also be used to Identify Monsters and Players you come across so that you know much more about them than you would otherwise know.

This is a very useful skill to have for those that are curious !

Skill Use
To identify Items use the Inventory screen and click the Identify button.

The Appraisal skill is used passively on the Info screen for Monsters and Players without you needing to click anything.
