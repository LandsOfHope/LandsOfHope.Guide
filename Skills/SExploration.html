Exploration
The Exploration skill is used to discover buildings and places that have been forgotten or lost for any number of reasons.

After you discover a lost Place it remains accessible for 1hr after which time it is forgotten again, each time you uncover a building your fame as an Explorer rises.

Skill Use
To use this skill goto a tile/location that you wish to explore then click the Explore button.

This will take you to the explore screen where you can discover strange and wonderous things.

You can also use the World > Exploration screen to find new places to explore.
