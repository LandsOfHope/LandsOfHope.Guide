Stolen Items
The Stolen Items screen lists all the items that have been stolen from you and that are still in existence in the world, this screen has no use in the non-grief server as you can not steal from players on the non-grief server however on the grief servers this screen can be used to try to figure out where your items went.

If you have items stolen there is no guarantee that whoever has them now will wish to return them, and the person holding the items now may or may not have anything to do with the person that originally stole them so its no good making threats or accusations unless you can really back your words up.

We will not restore stolen items to you if you are on the grief server as this is part of the risk of playing Lands of Hope.
