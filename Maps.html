Maps
Many of the Map screens in the game offer a quick and easy way to access a specific tile, generally clicking on a displayed position on a screen such as the World > Map screen will take you right to that tile.

In addition to this there is also the World screen, which shows a map of the entire known realms and lets you move across realms. This World map only shows you realms you have gained access to and will not show you all realms in the game unless you have unlocked them all.
